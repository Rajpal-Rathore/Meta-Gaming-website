Why $MetaSpace

Anti-Whale

All holders of MetaSpace will be protected from dumping & whales coming into the project attempting to own too large of a supply.We only want to attract firm believers in the project and steadily grow.

Marketing

The Space Invaders team is filled with well versed marketers that have worked on some of the largest projects in the BSC space. We're growing MetaSpace across all social media platforms.

Gaming

Play to earn gaming is the future and MetaSpace is striving to be a part of that future. We want to be a player in this new direction gaming is headed and that's why we have a team of 5 game developers.

Buy Back

A % of the tokenomics will be going to the buyback feature which the team will use to strategically pump or stabilize the chart when necessary.

Deflationary

1% of total supply burned every week to decrease the size of the circulating supply and to help PUMP the chart. Long term holders will be incentivized to hold.

Play To Earn

Holders of $MSPACE will be able to utilize their $MSPACE tokens to play our fun space invaders beta game! The gamer can either win or lose tokens based on skill level.

Burns

A portion of the tokens lost from the game will go into burn wallet & the other portion will go towards future features for the project.
